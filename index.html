Hello there! Welcome to my personal website - I'm absolutely thrilled to have you here! This is actually my first attempt at creating a website from scratch, so I'm really eager to share it with you, and I hope you enjoy what I've put together.

I'm Jun Chen (Chloe). Currently, I'm pursuing my PhD at Purdue University, focusing on Experience Design, guided by the expertise of Dr. Xinran Lehto. My research revolves around comprehending customers' behaviors and devising strategies to enhance their satisfaction. I must admit that I have a natural curiosity, strong critical thinking skills, and a deep sense of empathy, which allows me to put myself in customers' shoes, grasp their pain points, and craft innovative solutions. In my research, I love employing a multi-method approach. That means I'm just as comfortable crunching data using linear mixed-effects models as I am conducting insightful in-depth interviews.
