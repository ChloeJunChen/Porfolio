Welcome!


Thanks for stopping by. My name is Chloe (Jun Chen). I am a second-year Ph.D. student under the advisement of Dr. Xinran Lehto at Purdue University. My research focuses on consumer experience and consumer behavior, using both qualitative and quantitative methods. I have a strong interest in observing consumers. I seek to conduct interesting and sound science to modify consumers' behavior and improve consumers' experience. I am passionate about dance and badminton, and recently picked up the piano.
