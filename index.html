Hello there!


My name is Chloe, and I am a PhD student at Purdue University, under the advisement of Dr. Xinran Lehto. Through extensive training, I've developed expertise in understanding consumer's behavior and preferences. My research interests lie in understanding people's overall experience with products, from usage to satisfaction. I am naturally curious, possess strong critical thinking skills and deep empathy, which enables me to ask insightful questions. My research approach is multi-method, which means I am just as comfortable using linear mixed-effects models as I am conducting in-depth interviews. I look forward to connecting and exploring how we can work together to make a meaningful impact.
