Welcome!


Thanks for stopping by.

My name is Chloe. I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Purdue University. My research focuses on Experience Design and Experience Research, using both qualitative and quantitative methods. I have a keen interest in observing the psychology and behavior of consumers / users / guests. I seek to conduct interesting and sound science to improve their experience of life.
